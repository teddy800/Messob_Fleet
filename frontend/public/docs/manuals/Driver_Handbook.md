## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the MESSOB Fleet Management System Driver Handbook. As a driver, you are a critical part of our fleet operations, responsible for:

- **Safely transporting** passengers to their destinations
- **Maintaining assigned vehicles** in good condition
- **Following traffic laws** and organizational policies
- **Logging trip information** accurately in the system
- **Reporting issues** promptly to dispatchers
- **Providing excellent customer service** to passengers

This handbook will guide you through using the driver mobile app and help you perform your duties safely and efficiently.

## 2. Getting Started

### Logging In

**On Your Mobile Device:**
1. Open the **MESSOB Fleet Driver App**
2. Enter your credentials:
   - **Email:** Your assigned driver email
   - **Password:** Your secure password
3. Tap **"Sign In"**

**First Time Setup:**
- Enable **Location Services** (required for GPS tracking)
- Allow **Notifications** (for trip assignments)
- Test your **Internet connection** (Wi-Fi or Mobile Data)

### Driver App Overview

After login, you'll see:

- **My Trips:** Your assigned trips (upcoming, active, completed)
- **Quick Actions:** Start Trip, Log Fuel, Report Issue
- **Messages:** Communication from dispatchers
- **Profile:** Your information, license status, duty hours

   - **Pickup Location** and time

You'll receive push notifications for:
- ✅ New trip assignments
- ✅ Trip changes or cancellations

## 5. Starting a Trip

### Pre-Departure Checklist

**Before starting any trip:**

✅ **Vehicle Inspection:**
- Check fuel level (should be >25% for local, >50% for long trips)
- Inspect tires for proper inflation and wear
- Test lights (headlights, turn signals, brake lights)
- Check windshield wipers and washer fluid
- Verify cleanliness inside and outside
- Ensure no warning lights on dashboard

✅ **Documents:**
- Driver's license (must be current)
- Vehicle registration
- Insurance certificate
- Emergency contact card

✅ **Safety Equipment:**
- Spare tire and jack
- First aid kit
- Fire extinguisher
- Warning triangle
- Emergency phone numbers

⚠️ **If you find ANY safety issues, report immediately to dispatcher. Do NOT start trip.**

### Starting the Trip in the App

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Arrive at pickup location** on time (preferably 5 minutes early)
2. **Open the app** and locate the trip
3. **Tap "Start Trip"** button
4. System will:
   - Record start time
   - Capture current odometer reading (enter manually)
   - Begin GPS tracking
   - Notify passenger that you've arrived

5. **Verify passenger identity** (confirm name)
6. **Load any luggage** or equipment safely
7. **Ensure all passengers are seated** and buckled
8. **Begin driving** to destination

### What Gets Recorded

When you start a trip, the system logs:
- ⏰ Start time (exact timestamp)
- 📍 Start location (GPS coordinates)
- 🚗 Odometer reading at start
- ⛽ Fuel level at start
- 🗺️ Planned route to destination

## 6. During the Trip

### Safe Driving Practices

**Speed Limits:**
- Urban areas: 30-50 km/h (follow road signs)
- Highways: 80-100 km/h (as posted)
- **Never exceed posted limits**
- Adjust speed for weather, traffic, and road conditions

**Defensive Driving:**
- Maintain safe following distance (3-second rule)
- Anticipate other drivers' actions
- Avoid distractions (no phone use while driving)
- Stay alert and focused
- Use turn signals for all turns and lane changes

**Passenger Comfort:**
- Accelerate and brake smoothly
- Take corners at safe speeds
- Avoid sudden movements
- Keep cabin temperature comfortable
- Play radio at moderate volume (ask passenger preference)

### GPS Navigation

The app provides turn-by-turn navigation:

- 🗺️ Follow the suggested route (optimized by system)
- 📍 Blue line shows your planned route
- 🚗 Your current location updates every 5 seconds
- ⏱️ ETA updates automatically based on traffic

**If you need to deviate from the route:**
- Use your judgment for safety or traffic
- System will recalculate automatically
- Major deviations trigger alerts to dispatcher (they may call to verify)

### Communication

**With Passengers:**
- Greet professionally: "Good morning/afternoon, [Name]?"
- Confirm destination: "Taking you to [Location], correct?"
- Provide information: "Estimated drive time is [X] minutes"
- Be courteous and respectful at all times
- Ask if temperature/music is comfortable

**With Dispatchers:**
- Use the in-app chat for non-urgent matters
- Call directly for urgent issues or emergencies
- Respond promptly to messages
- Report significant delays immediately

### Handling Delays

**If you'll be more than 10 minutes late:**

1. Tap **"Report Delay"** in the app
2. Select reason:
   - Heavy traffic
   - Road closure/detour
   - Vehicle issue
   - Weather conditions
   - Passenger request (additional stop)
3. Enter estimated new arrival time
4. System notifies dispatcher and passenger automatically

### Fuel Management During Trip

**If fuel drops below 20%:**
- App will alert you to refuel
- **For long trips:** Plan fuel stops in advance
- **During trip:** If necessary, inform passenger of brief fuel stop
- Log all fuel purchases in the app (see Fuel Management section)

## 7. Completing a Trip

### End-of-Trip Checklist

When you reach the destination:

1. **Park safely** and legally
2. **Assist passengers** with exiting and unloading
3. **Ensure passengers haven't left belongings**
4. **Open the app** and tap **"Complete Trip"**

### Completing Trip in the App

**Step-by-Step:**

1. Tap **"Complete Trip"** button
2. **Enter final odometer reading**
3. **Enter current fuel level** (percentage or liters)
4. **Rate the trip** (optional):
   - Passenger behavior (professional/respectful)
   - Any issues or concerns
5. Add **notes** if needed:
   - Traffic conditions
   - Route changes
   - Passenger requests
   - Any incidents
6. Tap **"Submit"**

### What Gets Recorded

System logs:
- ⏰ End time (exact timestamp)
- 📍 End location (GPS coordinates)  
- 🚗 Final odometer reading
- ⛽ Fuel level at end
- 📊 Total distance traveled
- ⏱️ Total trip duration
- 💰 Estimated fuel cost

### Post-Trip Actions

**After completing a trip:**

✅ **Clean the vehicle** (remove any trash, check for lost items)
✅ **Check for damage** (report any new scratches, dents)
✅ **Return to base** (if end of shift) or **prepare for next trip**
✅ **Report any issues** via the app

## 8. Fuel Management

### When to Refuel

**Refuel when:**
- Fuel level drops below 25%
- Before long trips (ensure >50% fuel)
- At end of day if below 50% (for next driver)

**Where to Refuel:**
- Use **authorized fuel stations** only (list in app)
- Present company fuel card (if provided)
- Or keep receipt for cash reimbursement

### Logging Fuel Purchases

**Step-by-Step:**

1. Open app and tap **"Log Fuel"**
2. Enter details:
   - **Date & Time** (auto-filled)
   - **Fuel Station** (select from list or add)
   - **Odometer Reading**
   - **Fuel Amount** (liters)
   - **Cost** (in Ethiopian Birr)
   - **Receipt Number**
3. **Take photo of receipt** (tap camera icon)
4. Tap **"Submit"**

**Important:** All fuel purchases MUST be logged within 24 hours.

### Fuel Efficiency Tips

Help reduce fuel costs:

- ✅ Avoid idling for long periods
- ✅ Accelerate gradually
- ✅ Maintain steady speed (use cruise control on highways)
- ✅ Keep tires properly inflated
- ✅ Remove unnecessary weight from vehicle
- ✅ Use air conditioning sparingly
- ✅ Plan routes to avoid traffic

## 9. Vehicle Inspection

### Daily Pre-Trip Inspection

**Every morning before first trip:**

1. **Walk around vehicle:**
   - Check for body damage, scratches, dents
   - Inspect tires (tread depth, inflation, no punctures)
   - Look for fluid leaks under vehicle

2. **Check fluids:**
   - Engine oil (use dipstick)
   - Coolant level
   - Brake fluid
   - Windshield washer fluid

3. **Test all lights:**
   - Headlights (low and high beam)
   - Turn signals
   - Brake lights
   - Reverse lights
   - Interior lights

4. **Test wipers and horn**

5. **Check inside:**
   - Seat belts functional
   - Mirrors adjusted
   - First aid kit present
   - Fire extinguisher accessible

6. **Start engine:**
   - No warning lights on dashboard
   - Engine sounds normal
   - Brakes responsive

### Reporting Issues

**If you find ANY problem:**

1. Open app and tap **"Report Issue"**
2. Select vehicle
3. Choose issue category:
   - 🔴 Critical (unsafe to drive)
   - 🟠 Urgent (affects performance)
   - 🟡 Minor (cosmetic or comfort)
4. Describe the problem
5. **Take photos** if visible damage
6. Submit report

**For critical issues:**
- **DO NOT drive the vehicle**
- Call dispatcher immediately
- Wait for instructions
- Alternate vehicle will be assigned

### Weekly Inspection

In addition to daily checks:

- Clean interior thoroughly
- Vacuum floors and seats
- Wipe dashboard and windows
- Check spare tire pressure
- Inspect jack and tools
- Refill washer fluid
- Report need for car wash

## 10. Safety Guidelines

### General Safety Rules

**Always:**
- ✅ Wear seat belt (ensure all passengers buckled)
- ✅ Obey all traffic laws
- ✅ Follow speed limits
- ✅ Stop at red lights and stop signs
- ✅ Use turn signals
- ✅ Maintain safe following distance
- ✅ Stay in your lane
- ✅ Be courteous to other drivers

**Never:**
- ❌ Use mobile phone while driving
- ❌ Drive under influence of alcohol or drugs
- ❌ Drive when tired or drowsy
- ❌ Text or check social media
- ❌ Eat or drink while driving
- ❌ Smoke in the vehicle
- ❌ Play loud music
- ❌ Pick up unauthorized passengers

### Defensive Driving

- **Scan ahead:** Look 10-15 seconds ahead
- **Check mirrors:** Every 5-8 seconds
- **Watch for hazards:** Pedestrians, cyclists, children, animals
- **Plan escape routes:** Know where to go if someone cuts you off
- **Anticipate:** Expect other drivers to make mistakes

### Weather Conditions

**Rain:**
- Reduce speed by 10-15 km/h
- Increase following distance
- Turn on headlights
- Avoid sudden braking or turns
- Watch for standing water

**Fog:**
- Use low beam headlights (not high beams)
- Use fog lights if available
- Slow down significantly
- Use road edge markers as guide

**Night Driving:**
- Use headlights from dusk to dawn
- Reduce speed
- Be extra cautious at intersections
- Watch for pedestrians in dark clothing

### Passenger Safety

**Before departure:**
- Verify number of passengers matches trip request
- Ensure all have seat belts on
- Secure all luggage properly
- Brief passengers on estimated travel time

**During trip:**
- Drive smoothly (no sudden stops or turns)
- Avoid distractions from passengers
- Maintain professional boundary
- Stop only at designated points

## 11. Emergency Procedures

### Vehicle Breakdown

**If vehicle breaks down:**

1. **Pull over safely:**
   - Use hazard lights immediately
   - Move to shoulder or safe area
   - Turn off engine

2. **Ensure safety:**
   - Keep passengers inside (unless vehicle smoking/fire)
   - Place warning triangle 50m behind vehicle
   - Turn on hazard lights

3. **Contact dispatcher:**
   - Call emergency number
   - Explain the problem
   - Provide exact location
   - Estimate passenger count

4. **Update app:**
   - Tap "Report Issue"
   - Select "Breakdown"
   - Add details

5. **Wait for assistance:**
   - Stay with vehicle
   - Keep passengers calm
   - Follow dispatcher instructions

### Accident Procedure

**If involved in accident:**

1. **Stop immediately:**
   - Do NOT leave scene
   - Turn on hazard lights
   - Turn off engine

2. **Check for injuries:**
   - Assess yourself, passengers, other parties
   - Call 911/ambulance if anyone injured
   - Do NOT move injured persons (unless immediate danger)

3. **Call police:**
   - Always call police for accidents
   - Wait for officer to arrive
   - Get police report number

4. **Document scene:**
   - Take photos of damage (all vehicles)
   - Photo of license plates
   - Photo of road conditions
   - Get witness names and contacts

5. **Exchange information:**
   - Driver names and contacts
   - Insurance information
   - Vehicle registration numbers
   - DO NOT admit fault

6. **Notify dispatcher immediately:**
   - Call emergency hotline
   - Explain what happened
   - Follow instructions

7. **Complete accident report in app:**
   - Detailed description
   - Attach all photos
   - Submit witness information

### Medical Emergency

**If passenger has medical emergency:**

1. **Pull over safely**
2. **Call 911/ambulance** immediately
3. **Provide first aid** if trained and safe to do so
4. **Contact dispatcher**
5. **Stay with passenger** until help arrives
6. **Document in app** after situation resolved

### Security Threats

**If you feel threatened:**

1. **Stay calm**
2. **Drive to public area** (police station, hospital, busy area)
3. **Lock doors**
4. **Call dispatcher or police**
5. **Do NOT confront** or argue

⚠️ **Your safety comes first. Property can be replaced, you cannot.**

## 12. Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What if I'm going to be late for pickup?

**A:** Contact dispatcher immediately via phone or app. They will notify the passenger. Document the reason for delay in the system.

### Q: Can I let someone else drive the vehicle?

**A:** Absolutely not. Only authorized, assigned drivers may operate fleet vehicles. This is a serious policy violation.

### Q: What if passenger requests a detour or additional stop?

**A:** Contact dispatcher first for approval. If approved, they'll update the trip. Log the extra distance in your completion notes.

### Q: What should I do if I find lost items in the vehicle?

**A:** Report via app immediately. Turn in items to dispatcher or lost & found. System will help match items to passengers.

### Q: Can I use the vehicle for personal errands?

**A:** No. Fleet vehicles are for official trips only. Personal use is strictly prohibited and may result in termination.

### Q: What if I get a traffic ticket while on duty?

**A:** Report to dispatcher immediately. Provide ticket details. Depending on circumstances, you may be responsible for fine.

### Q: How do I request time off?

**A:** Submit leave request through HR system at least 2 weeks in advance. Emergency leave should be reported to dispatcher immediately.

### Q: What's the maximum hours I can drive per day?

**A:** Maximum 10 hours of driving per day with required rest breaks. System tracks your hours automatically.

### Q: What if vehicle needs washing?

**A:** Report in app. Dispatcher will schedule car wash at authorized facility. Keep receipt if you're instructed to wash vehicle.

### Q: Can passengers sit in front seat?

**A:** Generally, passengers should sit in rear seats unless vehicle is full or passenger has mobility issues requiring front seat access.
